## 🏛️ Regulatory State Compliance & Lapses
Auto insurance parameters in the US are heavily monitored at the state level by bodies such as the [California Department of Insurance](https://ca.gov) and the National Association of Insurance Commissioners ([NAIC](https://naic.org)). 

Failing to submit payments on schedule or letting an active vehicle contract drop into a **lapse state** triggers immediate administrative fines (ranging from $10 to $25) and puts the driver at risk of immediate registration suspension by their local DMV network. Always verify account balances directly on official systems to protect continuous tenure-based loyalty metrics.
